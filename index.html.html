Fun Facts

  • Fastest Super Bowl Success: Founded in 1996, the Ravens won their first Super Bowl (XXXV) in the 2000 season just five years after their inception.
  • Undefeated in Super Bowls: The Ravens are one of the few NFL teams with multiple Super Bowl appearances and zero losses (2-0).
  • Record-Setting Defense: The 2000 Ravens defense allowed only 165 points, the fewest in a 16-game season.
  • The "Raven" Name: The team name was chosen via a Baltimore Sun telephone poll that received a record number of calls supporting the name inspired by Edgar Allan Poe.
  • Historic Draft: The team’s first-ever draft picks, Jonathan Ogden (1996) and Ray Lewis (1996), are both Pro Football Hall of Famers.

Ravens Current Logo

Ravens Current Logo Description

  • The current logo of the Baltimore Ravens features a fierce, stylized raven’s head in profile, facing right. The bird is primarily black and purple, with gold and white accents that highlight its sharp beak and intense eye, giving it a bold and aggressive look. A large “B” is placed prominently on the raven’s head, representing Baltimore. The logo reflects strength, speed, and the team’s defensive legacy, making it instantly recognizable and symbolizing the Ravens’ identity both on and off the field.
